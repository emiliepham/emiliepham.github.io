Education

I lived most of my life in a small town called Franconville, not too far away from Paris.
I have studied in my hometown high school, except for my last year of high school where I went to Canberra, Australia to pass my Scientific French Baccalaureate (French equivalent for A levels), with Mathematics specialty. It was a pretty big change but I liked it and I had the chance to be in a very small class of ten students, since the French system isn't that popular in Canberra.

Once I got my French Bac, I decided to go back home for university and started studying a Bachelor in Economics and Business Management at the Sorbonne University, which I didn't enjoy that much. I finished my first year, but I decided I no longer wanted to study these subjects.

I then decided to move to London and start my BSc Mathematics at King's College London, which I should graduate from in 2017.
